Overview

For over ten years I have worked alongside artists, curators, educators and scientists to broaden public access to culture and knowledge, and create engaging programs that deepen the public's connection to contemporary matters.

Past Highlights

  • Originated the Digital Engagement department for the Pérez Art Museum Miami. Our efforts in digital art were profiled by PBS in 2024.
  • I was profiled in Internet_Art: From the Birth of the Web to the Rise of NFTs, published by Phaidon in 2023.
  • In 2024 I was asked to present at Gray Area's Festival 10 alongside a cohort of figures in new media art and technology. My talk addressed the widening gap between cultural organizations and their audiences due to their failure to adapt to modern technologies and mediums.
  • In 2023 we launched PAMMTV, the first ever streaming service for video art.
  • I won a Webby Award for Send Me SFMOMA, an SMS service that allowed anyone in the US to search for art using everyday language.
